Mountain Adventures

Conquer Africa's highest peaks and experience breathtaking alpine landscapes

Mount Kilimanjaro

Africa's highest peak at 5,895 meters. Challenge yourself with this iconic climb through diverse ecosystems, from tropical rainforest to arctic summit.

Height: 5,895m (19,341ft)
Duration: 5-9 days
Difficulty: Moderate to Challenging

Mount Kenya

The second-highest peak in Africa at 5,199 meters. Experience technical climbing and stunning alpine scenery in this UNESCO World Heritage site.

Height: 5,199m (17,057ft)
Duration: 4-6 days
Difficulty: Challenging
